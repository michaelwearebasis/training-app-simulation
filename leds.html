On each circuit

Every circuit has one small light on the switchboard. Here's what it's telling you.
Solid red
Live — the circuit is on and power is available.
Slowly pulsing red
Standby — switched off from the app, ready to come back on.
Off
Safe — the circuit is off, or locked out. Nothing live.
Solid yellow
Electrical fault — the circuit has tripped (RCD, MCB or overcurrent).
Blinking blue
Change pending — a new setting or update is waiting to be applied.
Blinking yellow
System fault — the circuit module needs attention.
On a circuit, red is normal — it just means the power is on. Yellow is the colour to watch.

On the panel's brain

The System Manager — the module that runs the panel — has two lights.
Status light
Gently breathing white
All good — the panel is running normally.
Blinking blue
Press the Basis Button — to confirm a new panel or apply your changes.
Blinking white
Starting up.
Fast blinking yellow
Updating the panel's software — leave it be for a minute.
Comms light
Solid white
Online — connected to the internet.
Blinking white
Connecting to the network…
Solid blue
A phone is connected to the panel directly.
Blinking blue
The panel's hotspot is on, waiting for a phone to join.
Blinking yellow
Couldn't connect to the network.
Off
No network set up, or the panel is off.
Blue = a change or a phone · Yellow = something to check · White = healthy · Red = a circuit's power (normal) · Off = nothing live
