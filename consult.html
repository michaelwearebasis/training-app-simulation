A Basis Panel is only as good as the conversation you have first.

Before you quote, sit down with the customer and work through these six questions. Each answer changes how you set up the board — and the wiring.

1What they want

What are they chasing?

Insights only, or control and automation too? Lower bills, solar optimisation, safety — or all three?

Remember — their answer shapes the whole board layout.

How will they live with it?

Remote control from the app, routines that shift load off-peak, standby on circuits they rarely use.

Remember — match the layout to how they live, not just code minimums.
2Planning the circuits

What gets its own circuit?

Anything they want to see, control, or automate: HWC, EV charger, pool or spa pump, oven, heat pumps.

Watch out — group these onto shared circuits and you take those options away.

What must stay on 24/7?

Fridges, freezers, security, medical equipment, and the router or ONT — never switchable off.

Remember — agree it up front and lock it in. Everything else can be controllable.
3Before install day

Ethernet or Wi-Fi?

Hardwire Ethernet to the board if you can. Wi-Fi works — but check the signal actually reaches the board first.

Watch out — Wi-Fi is our customers' most common problem.

Is the existing wiring up to it?

Check condition, capacity, and supply limits before you commit.

Remember — flag anything below standard now, so there are no surprises on install day.
